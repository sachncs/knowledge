# Parting Words

**BE CONSISTENT.**

If you're editing code, take a few minutes to look at the code around you and determine its style. If they use `_idx` suffixes in index variable names, you should too.

The point of having style guidelines is to have a common vocabulary of coding so people can concentrate on what you're saying rather than on how you're saying it.

## Consistency is the ultimate rule

Most local style rules can be overridden by consistency with the surrounding code. When in doubt, use your best judgment. Look at other examples and decide what looks best. And ask questions.
