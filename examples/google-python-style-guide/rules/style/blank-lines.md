## Summary

Two blank lines between top-level definitions, one blank line between method definitions.

Two blank lines between top-level definitions (function or class definitions). One blank line between method definitions and between the docstring of a class and the first method. No blank line following a `def` line. Use single blank lines as appropriate within functions or methods.
